Data Saving
This web site saves data in a way that you're probably not used to. There are no Submit or Save buttons. Data is saved automatically every time you move from field to field and from page to page. This unique behavior is designed to prevent data loss when entering information on long data-entry forms.

Submission Lifecycle and Status Codes
WCF uses status codes to manage the submit/review/revise/accept and publish lifecycle. Each submission's status is shown on the My Submissions page, and beneath the Submission ID in the box of navigator links on the top left of every page when you're viewing and editing the submission.
